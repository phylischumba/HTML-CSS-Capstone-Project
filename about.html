HOME SCHOOLERS MEETUP EVENT IN JULY 2020

Home schooling is education provided at home or any other place that isn't a school. It is meant to provide more attention and help nurture the learners heart desires with the help of a parent or tutor.

The meetup is meant for the learners and their guardians. It will give them an opportunity to socialize and exchange ideas amongst them and other attendants, as well as interact with experts within and outside the country.

Please contact us for any further questions about the Home schooling meetup:
organizers@homeschoolers.org

Home Schoolers Meetup 2020 Logo

The Home schoolers meetup event was all created during a science contest in January 2019 after organizers realised that homeschooling is left out.
Organizers therefore came up with an idea of having a meetup event specifically for homeschooling parents/guardians and the learners for socialization intentions.
